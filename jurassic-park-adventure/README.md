# Jurassic Park Adventure

A hand-drawn storybook by four elementary school friends.

## The Story

Four friends — Alina, Quinn, Joli, and Riley — find themselves on a wild adventure with dinosaurs. Together with a dinosaur named Blue, they face volcanic eruptions, underwater rescues, T-Rex attacks, and mysterious nightmares. Will they make it out alive?

37 pages of original hand-drawn illustrations in colored pencil and crayon.
